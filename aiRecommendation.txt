On this QTDB record, that is essentially **perfect**.

Now, one caveat:

* This is **one record**, not the entire QTDB.
* Pan-Tompkins is known to perform exceptionally well on clean databases like QTDB and MIT-BIH, but performance can drop on noisy ambulatory recordings.

So before claiming "100% QRS detection," we'd want to repeat this across all validation records.

But if it stays above **99.5%**, then for *your application* it becomes an extremely strong anchor.
